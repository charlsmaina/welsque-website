As Welsque Limited, we operate across various sectors, providing tailored security solutions to meet diverse needs. Discover how we cater to each sector to ensure safety and protection.

Businesses

We provide comprehensive security solutions for businesses of all sizes, ensuring the safety of employees, assets, and premises. Our services include manned guarding, surveillance, and emergency response.

College Campus Guarding

Our security services for college campuses are designed to protect students, faculty, and property. We offer on-site security officers, patrol services, and emergency response planning.

Hospitals

In hospitals, we focus on protecting patients, staff, and facilities. Our services include access control, patient security, and emergency response coordination to ensure a safe healthcare environment.

Construction Sites

We safeguard construction sites to prevent theft, vandalism, and unauthorized access. Our security solutions include site patrols, monitoring, and access control systems.

Warehouses

Our warehouse security services aim to protect inventory, equipment, and facilities. We offer surveillance, manned guarding, and alarm monitoring to ensure continuous protection.

Road Construction Materials Guarding

We provide security for road construction materials to prevent theft and damage. Our services include on-site monitoring, patrols, and inventory management.

Consulting

Welsque Limited offers consulting services to assess security needs and develop tailored solutions. Our consultants work closely with clients to identify vulnerabilities and recommend effective security measures.
