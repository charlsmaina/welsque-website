About Us

We are an acclaimed security firm established in Kenya. We are established by highly qualified and experienced individuals combining experiences from Security. We are capable of dealing with all your security needs and offer a complete range, planning, system analysis and design as well as executive services. We are committed to providing highest quality of security and protection services to individuals and businesses. We set ourselves apart from the competition by custom designing to meet and exceed the specific demands and expectations of our clients. In doing so, we hand-pick highly experienced personnels and also, we provide meticulous training and ensure that our protective officers are fully bonded, insured and licensed. Our mission is to provide the highest quality security experience to our clients, with a great emphasis on customer service and the protection of people, property, and possessions. We provide qualified staffs that have the background, education and experience to ensure high level of security at any event or situation. We are committed to helping our clients build organizations that are secure in an age of ever-changing security risks. The insight and depth of experience we have gained are invaluable in providing security professionalism needed to grasp opportunities and manage risk with greater certainty. We never lose sight of the fact that we are in the business of protecting people, not just property. Our approach is all about being a security services provider that our customers can trust.
